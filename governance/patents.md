# Patent Non-Assertion Pledge: The Loom Method

A governance instrument of The Loom Foundation, adopted for The Loom Method and inherited by each of
its parts. It covers The Loom Method and all of its parts, and any independent implementation.

## The pledge

Bruno Almeida do Lago, and any future Loom Foundation to which the project is transferred (together,
the "Pledgor"), pledge not to assert any patent they own or control against any party for **making,
using, selling, offering to sell, importing, or otherwise practising or implementing The Loom Method
or any of its parts**, including any independent implementation of the methodology and any use,
modification, or distribution of its tools, to the extent the patent claim would necessarily be
infringed by doing so.

This pledge is irrevocable except as stated under *Defensive termination*, and it runs with any
patent it covers: it remains in force even if that patent is later sold or transferred.

## Defensive termination

The pledge is withdrawn with respect to any party that initiates, funds, or voluntarily joins a
patent claim (other than a counterclaim or other defensive response) against the Pledgor, or against
any user, contributor, distributor, or implementer of The Loom Method or any of its parts, alleging
that The Loom Method, a part, or an implementation infringes a patent. Withdrawal is limited to that
party.

## Relationship to the licenses

This pledge is in addition to, and does not limit, the rights granted by the licenses on the works
themselves. Each part of The Loom Method is licensed in its own repository. Where a license grants
broader patent rights, that grant controls.

## Scope

This pledge concerns patents only. It grants no copyright or trademark rights, and places no
obligation on the artifacts you author by applying the method or running a tool; those are entirely
yours.
